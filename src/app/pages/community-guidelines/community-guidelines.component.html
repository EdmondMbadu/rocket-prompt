Community Guidelines & Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: {{ today }}

Welcome to RocketPrompt.io.

Our goal is to provide a creative, safe, and collaborative environment where users can create, share, and explore prompts that inspire positive, constructive outcomes.

By using RocketPrompt.io, you agree to follow the rules outlined below. These guidelines apply to all content you create, upload, save, share, or distribute through this platform.

1. Allowed Content

You are free to create and share prompts that:

  • Encourage learning, creativity, curiosity, and exploration
  • Support personal development and skill-building
  • Promote collaboration, reflection, or healthy discussion
  • Are respectful and considerate of individuals and communities

We encourage prompts that are:

  • Helpful
  • Educational
  • Positive
  • Safe
  • Inclusive

2. Strictly Prohibited Content

You may not create, store, or share any prompt that directly or indirectly encourages, instructs, glorifies, or supports harmful or illegal activity.

Examples include (but are not limited to):

Dangerous or Violent Acts

  • Building weapons, explosives, bombs, or harmful devices
  • Violent threats or instructions to harm oneself or others
  • Prompts that advocate suicide, self-harm, or injury

Sexual Exploitation or Abuse

  • Sexual content involving minors or any non-consensual act
  • Prompts encouraging rape, coercion, or sexual violence
  • Any form of sexual harassment or predatory behavior

Hate or Discrimination

  • Prompts that attack or demean groups based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, identity, nationality, or disability
  • Advocacy for segregation, violence, or exclusion of protected groups

Fraud, Theft, or Criminal Activity

  • Prompts teaching scams, fraud, or deception
  • Bypassing security systems, hacking, or digital intrusion
  • Money laundering, identity theft, tax evasion or similar crimes

Misinformation & Harmful Manipulation

  • Encouraging conspiracies presented as fact
  • Medical or legal instructions that can cause harm
  • Content intended to mislead or manipulate individuals or groups

If you are unsure whether something is allowed, assume it is not allowed.

3. Respect & Responsibility

You are responsible for:

  • The prompts you create
  • The content you share
  • The impact your content may have

Do not use this platform to harass, threaten, exploit, shame, or intimidate anyone.

4. Reporting & Moderation

We actively moderate content to ensure platform safety.

We reserve the right to:

  • Review content at any time
  • Remove or restrict content that violates these guidelines
  • Suspend or permanently disable accounts that repeatedly or severely violate these rules

If you see content that violates these terms, please report it to our moderation team.

5. No Liability for User Content

Content on this platform is created by users.

We do not endorse or verify the accuracy or safety of any prompt.

You use shared prompts at your own discretion and responsibility.

6. Amendment of Terms

These guidelines may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the platform means you agree to the most current version.

Summary

  • Create and share positive and constructive prompts
  • Do not create harmful, violent, abusive, hateful, or illegal prompts
  • We may remove content and suspend accounts that violate these rules

We appreciate your cooperation in keeping this community safe, creative, and inspiring.
