Rocket Prompt - AI Prompt Library for Teams

Rocket Prompt (RocketPrompt) is a prompt library and management platform for AI teams. Store, organize, share, and optimize your AI prompts across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and other LLMs in one collaborative workspace.

Built for teams that rely on AI daily, Rocket Prompt helps you stop losing your best prompts in chat history. Create a shared prompt library, version your prompts, and track which prompts deliver the best results.

What You Can Do with Rocket Prompt

  • Build a shared AI prompt library for your entire team.
  • Organize prompts by project, department, or use case.
  • Version and iterate on prompts to improve AI output quality.
  • Share proven prompts across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and more.
  • Track prompt performance and find what works best.
